About ALHADY

Built on
Engineering
Excellence.

ALHADY is a specialized Egyptian engineering company delivering premium aluminum systems, high-performance facades, and architectural glass solutions for residential and commercial projects.

With our factory in Abu Rawash and our office in Omraniya, we combine industrial-scale manufacturing with hands-on project precision — from design consultation through final installation.

Every system we build is engineered to perform structurally, to last climatically, and to look exceptional architecturally.
